Frequently Asked Questions

What types of panels can RIVET analyze?

RIVET works with PLCs (Allen-Bradley, Siemens, Schneider), motor control centers, distribution panels, VFDs, and most industrial electrical equipment. If it has a nameplate or visible components, we can analyze it.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

RIVET uses Claude Sonnet 4, trained on millions of technical documents and images. It identifies components with high accuracy and provides practical troubleshooting steps. However, always verify critical decisions with your own expertise and safety procedures.

Why Telegram instead of a website or app?

Telegram works everywhere - factory floors, basements, anywhere you have cell service. No app downloads, no logins to remember, no complicated UI. Just snap and send. Plus, you have a full history of all your analyses in one chat.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes. Photos are processed securely and not shared with third parties. We store anonymized analytics to improve the service, but your specific images and conversations remain private. You can delete your data anytime by contacting support.
